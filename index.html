About me

Hi, I’m Charifa—a curious and passionate software engineer in the making. I thrive on solving complex problems, exploring new technologies, and transforming ideas into working solutions. My journey in programming began with a fascination for how things work and has evolved into a love for building systems that are both functional and elegant.
I enjoy diving into both front-end and back-end web development, experimenting with frameworks and languages.Over time, I have gained hands-on experience with databases and tools.
Beyond coding, I love reading, crocheting, and expanding my skills across multiple disciplines. I’m fascinated by artificial intelligence, game development, and designing software that makes life easier or more fun. I believe in continuous learning, creativity, and structured thinking.
Whether it’s building a new application, optimizing existing systems, or experimenting with cutting-edge technologies, I find joy in the process of learning, creating, and improving. My goal is to grow into a well-rounded engineer who can bring innovative ideas to life while leaving a positive impact through technology.

Education

My academic journey began at EST Essaouira, where I earned my DUT in Génie Informatique with distinction (mention Très Bien). These two years introduced me to programming, databases, web development, and computer architecture, and they helped shape my curiosity for software engineering.

After succeeding in the national entrance exam, I joined ENSA Safi as a first-year engineering student in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence. Through this path, I’m developing stronger skills in algorithms, system design, AI fundamentals, and full-stack development while working on both academic and personal projects.

Education plays a major role in my growth, and I continue to strengthen it with self-learning, projects, and hands-on experience.

Projects

My work spans full-stack development, software engineering, and early AI exploration. For my PFE, I built a complete e-learning platform for computer science students using Spring Boot, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, and PostgreSQL, delivering a structured and scalable application.

For my SFE, I developed an AI-based project using Python and Flask, which introduced me to model integration and API-driven design. I’ve also worked on several other projects, including a JavaFX desktop management app, multiple web applications with Laravel and JavaScript, a task management system, and small game projects like Pac-Man.

Each project helped me explore new technologies, improve problem-solving skills, and strengthen my engineering mindset.
